Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Spendly ("we", "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our personal expense tracking application ("Service"). By using Spendly, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Data We Collect

To provide the Service, we collect the following types of information:

  • Account details you provide, such as your name and email address
  • Expense data you enter, including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions
  • Basic usage information, such as login timestamps, needed to keep your account secure

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect to:

  • Operate and maintain your account and expense records
  • Generate spending summaries, category breakdowns, and other insights within the app
  • Communicate with you about your account or important updates to the Service
  • Improve the reliability and usability of Spendly

We do not use your financial data for advertising purposes, and we do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely and is only accessible to you through your authenticated account. We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly may rely on trusted third-party providers for functions such as hosting, authentication, or analytics. These providers only receive the information necessary to perform their function and are not permitted to use your data for any other purpose. We do not share your expense data with advertisers or data brokers.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the contact details provided within the app.
